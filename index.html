The Joy of Travel

About Me

I have lived in many places and traveled to many others. Programming is amazing and fun. Blogging, however, is much simpler and boring.

Reasons to Travel:

  • Travel opens one's eyes to different ways of life
  • Travel makes one realize how culture affects your way of thinking
  • It is amazing to see other cities and other countries
  • Travel can be relaxing
